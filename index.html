Our Commitment to Sri Lanka

In light of the recent devastating floods affecting our beautiful country, we have made a firm commitment: 10% of every single purchase on our platform will be donated to the Sri Lanka Flood Relief Fund.

This initiative is our way of standing in solidarity with our fellow Sri Lankans who have lost homes, belongings, and loved ones. We believe that businesses have a responsibility to support their communities in times of crisis.

Your purchase doesn't just get you premium electronics - it helps provide:

  • Emergency food supplies
  • Clean drinking water
  • Medical assistance
  • Temporary shelter
  • Rebuilding support

Shop with purpose. Your purchase makes a real difference.
